Jin and Qi thus became powerful states. In Duke Wu's nineteenth year   the ruler of Quwoin Jin for the  first time  assumed the  title  of marquis  of  Jin. Duke Huan of Qi became an overlord (ba) at Juan. Duke Wu of Qin died in his twentieth year of rule and was buried at Pingyang in Yong.  For  the  first time there were persons to accompany him in death, in this case sixty-six persons.
